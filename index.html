Education Background

I spent my formative years in Kosamba, Gujarat, where I received my early education at Public School Kharod, Ankleshwar, from nursery through class 5. Later, I transitioned to Aditya Birla Public School, Kharach, and continued my academic journey there until class 8. The pursuit of knowledge led me to Surat, where I enrolled in Delhi Public School, Tapi, and completed my secondary education up to class 10.

In the pursuit of academic excellence, I decided to specialize in the Science (Mathematics) stream during my 11th-grade studies. Eager to excel in competitive examinations, I joined the prestigious Allen Institute to prepare for the challenging JEE Exam. My association with Allen tuitions commenced in class 9, providing me with a strong foundation and valuable insights into the world of mathematics and sciences. This educational journey has equipped me with a diverse set of skills and knowledge, laying the groundwork for future academic and professional endeavors.

Achievements

Embarking on a journey of continuous achievement and excellence, my ventures in diverse fields have shaped my educational odyssey. As a proficient martial artist, I proudly held the esteemed rank of a purple belt in Taekwondo, showcasing my prowess by clinching medals in both kata and kumite competitions. My early years were adorned with a multitude of accolades, including gold in a dance competition during my seventh-grade, demonstrating a multifaceted talent.

Adding intellectual feats to my repertoire, I actively participated in the Science Olympiad Foundation (SOF) Olympiads until class 8, consistently earning medals for my commendable performance. The year of class 8 saw me stepping into the tech-savvy arena, as I engaged in the TCS IT Wiz, a prestigious computer and technology quiz competition. The subsequent year witnessed my foray into anchoring, showcasing my versatility and communication skills.

In the pursuit of academic excellence, I undertook the Late KVPY exam during class 11, securing an impressive All India Rank of 2000. My commitment to academic prowess continued into class 10, where I achieved an outstanding score of 95.6% in the board examinations.

The pinnacle of my academic journey was marked by my exceptional performance in the JEE MAINS during class 12, securing an All India Rank 889. I gave JEE ADVANCED and secured All India Rank 3189. Simultaneously, I achieved a remarkable 93.4% in the class 12 board examinations, affirming my commitment to both intellectual and academic pursuits. These accomplishments stand as a testament to my holistic approach to education and my unwavering dedication to excellence.
